General Settings
================

- Insert images to the field “Image file(s) for full background,
  slideshow or banner”If you don't activate the slideshow under the tab
  “slideshow” only the first image will be used.

- Optionally define the captions in “Slide Captions” (one caption per
  line). You have to activate the slideshow and you have to “Enable
  slide captions (slide\_captions)” under tab “Slideshow” if you want to
  show them.

- Optionally define the weblinks in “Link images to following URL” (one
  link per line)

- All other settings in this tab are optionally. Normally you should
  make this settings with TypoScript in your template (chapter
  “Configuration) and leave this fields empty. But you can override the
  TypoScript settings here in special cases.
